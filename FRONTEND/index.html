Who We Are
About JYC

JUIT Youth Club (JYC) is the hub of enthusiasm and growth, expanding students' horizons beyond academics into sports, literature, culture, and technology.

It fosters self-expression, recognition, and integration while building teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership. By organizing events and extracurricular activities, JYC provides a refreshing break from academic pressures, instilling confidence and a sense of achievement.
